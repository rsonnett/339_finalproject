What is the Dictionary Definition of Dance?

Dance is defined as "to move one's body rythmically, usually to music according to Webster's Dictionary.

What are Dancers' Definition of Dance?

"For me, dance is a form of meditation. It's a state of mindfulness where I lose myself in the present moment, fully immersed in the rhythm and flow of movement." - Sabrina Chorna

"Dance is my happy place, where I can be fully myself. In the real world, I constantly overthink, and am not fully confidence, but dance allows me to be unapologetically myself." - Hadley McElroy

"Dance is the language of my soul, where every movement speaks volumes that words cannot express. No matter what I am feepng, I am able to express it best through dance." - Jenna Barad

Dance holds an immense significance in the lives of people worldwide, spanning across numerous cultures and communities. With an ever-expanding array of genres continually emerging, the world of dance remains vibrant and dynamic. For me, as well as countless others, dance transcends mere hobby; it is a profound passion. Explore the diverse array of dance styles and discover some of my personal favorites.
